Our Mission

The mission of Baylor Baja is not all about beating the competition. The main objective is to provide Baylor engineering students the opportunity to learn how mechanical and electrical systems work outside of the classroom. Building an off-road performance vehicle is the perfect way to do so. Engineering students gain invaluable hands-on experience in both design and fabrication, all the while learning how systems can come together to develop into a whole. Baylor Baja is dedicated to showing the nation the quality of students that go to Baylor and preparing each individual for any adversity that may come his or her way. Our sponsors and other companies that have connections to SAE Baja will see that Baylor University produces quality students and will hopefully see what value those students can bring to their companies, resulting in post-college opportunities for the students involved.
